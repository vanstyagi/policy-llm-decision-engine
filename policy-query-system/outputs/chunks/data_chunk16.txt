Obesity is measure d in Body Mass Index. Body mass index (BMI) is a simple index of weight -for-height that is commonly used to classify overweight and obesity in adults. It is defined as a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of his height in meters (kg/m2). The WHO definition is:  BMI greater than or equal to 25 is overweight  BMI greater than or equal to 30 is obesity 31.
